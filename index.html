You have 3 types of troops:
archers - low health, shoot.
  • wars - a lot of health, close combat.
  • mage - casts a magic barrier that stops the enemy and deals small damage.
    • To cast a barrier, press the right mouse button and then left button above the place where you want to place the barrier
    To remove a building/warrior or undo a spell use the cross in the top panel.

  • house - brings money (to collect taxes, hover over a coin above the house)
